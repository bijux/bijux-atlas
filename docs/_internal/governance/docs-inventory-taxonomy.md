## Categories

- `Governance`: contributor-only rules, ownership, validation, and publication controls.
- `Reference`: stable definitions, commands, schemas, and factual lookup pages.
- `Runbook`: operator procedures with explicit verification and rollback steps.

## Placement Rule

- Put policy and enforcement material under `docs/_internal/governance/`.
- Put stable facts under `docs/reference/`.
- Put operator procedures under `docs/operations/` and `docs/operations/runbooks/`.
