# ADR Template: SLA Decision

## Title

- `ADR-YYYYMMDD-sla-decision`

## Context

- Why an external SLA is being considered now.

## Decision

- SLA scope and mapped SLO ids.
- Availability/success/latency objectives and windows.
- Explicit exclusions list.

## Consequences

- Operational costs.
- Required alerting/runbook changes.
- Customer-facing communication impact.

## Validation

- Evidence artifacts and reporting pipeline used for SLA proof.
