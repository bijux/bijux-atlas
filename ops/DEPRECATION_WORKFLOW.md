# Deprecation Workflow

- Authority Tier: `tier2`
- Audience: `contributors`
- Owner: `bijux-atlas-operations`
- Purpose: `standardize deprecation notices, cutover windows, and removal criteria for ops contracts and docs`
- Consumers: `checks_ops_human_workflow_maturity`

## Workflow
- Declare deprecation target and replacement.
- Define cutoff date and compatibility window.
- Update references and migration notes.
- Validate consumers are migrated before removal.
- Remove deprecated surface and close tracking notes.

## Required Metadata
- Deprecation Target
- Replacement Path
- Cutoff Date
- Consumer Impact
